# Hi, I'm WinterCG 👋

I'm a **3D artist** with professional experience in game art, architectural visualization, and material authoring. That's my main field and my craft.

Game development started as a hobby, but it has become something I take seriously. I'm now shipping real projects with a lot of effort behind them, and I've built production workflows around AI and coding agents to design, prototype, and iterate far faster than I could alone.

I like working end to end: from 3D and procedural art through to gameplay systems and the tooling that ties them together.

## 🎮 Projects I'm shipping

**Siegecrawl** &nbsp;`Godot 4.6`
A real-time, deck-driven tower defense roguelike. Towers grow by fighting and shrink by suffering as you hold a spiral road against a counterattacking dungeon.

**Stridekeep** &nbsp;`Godot 4.6`
A puzzle roguelike that blends Puzzle Bobble, Pachinko, and Balatro into one chain reaction.

**Witclash** &nbsp;`TypeScript`
An AI-judged debate arena where craft beats correctness. Players make their case and an AI scores the rhetoric.

**MarbleKeep** &nbsp;`Godot 4.6`
A cozy, always-on-top desktop toy. A physics marble rolls in the corner of your screen while you collect procedurally generated marbles across four rarities.

**Breakout Roguelike** &nbsp;`Godot 4.6` &nbsp;[source](https://github.com/wynterCG/BreakoutRoguelike)
Breakout crossed with roguelike run structure and upgrades.
